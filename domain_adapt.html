Domain Adaptation Of LLM's for low resource industrial text data(Currently ongoing)

  • Home

Given enough domain specific data, Large Language models can be adapted to perform much better that of the shelf language model on the underlying task belonging to that particular domain. With this research project we investigate whether it is still helpful to try to domain adapt these models even when the available pre-training data is very small in size. Further we try to investigate the impact of vocabulary overlap i.e. the extent to which vocabulary of the original language model overlap with the vocabulary of the task data and the nature of in-domain data on the effectiveness of domain adaptation of large language models. This Project is part of my master's thesis done at Siemens.

Complete details will be available once the project is finished.
